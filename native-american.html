Native Americans

the native Amricans consist of all the tribes that inhibited the American continent before the arrival of the Europeans. Over the centuries since then, the Native Americans experienced much hate and displacement throughout the development of America. The Trail of Tears was the largest displacement of Natives in american history. Approximately 60,000 Native Americans, mostly Cherokee , were forcefully displaced between the 1830’s to 1850”s.
