Mexican-American War

Around 1820, settlers from America started going to what is now Texas. In 1836, the Americans in Texas decided enough was enough. They declared independence from Mexico. Mexico didn't like the idea of their independence but there was nothing they could do. Mexico decided Texas could be its own nation, as long as it didn't apply to join the US. After Texas became a sovereign nation (called the Lone Star Republic) they applied for statehood. Mexico said that if the US annexed them, it would be an act of war. And so, the Mexican-American war began.
